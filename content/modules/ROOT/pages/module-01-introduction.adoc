= Introduction: Models-as-a-Service

When AI infrastructure is self-service without guardrails, problems emerge: teams duplicate model deployments unnecessarily, GPUs sit idle or overload, and costs escalate without accountability.

[.bordershadow]
image::iaas.png[width="50%"]

**Models-as-a-Service** solves this by centralizing model deployment and exposing them through secure, authenticated APIs. Platform teams manage models once; developers consume them without GPU complexity.

[.bordershadow]
image::maas.png[width="50%"]

== What You'll Experience

In this 10-15 minute workshop, you'll:

1. **Get model credentials** from a centrally-deployed NVIDIA Nemotron model
2. **Use agentic AI** to interact with Rocket.Chat using natural language
3. **View usage analytics** showing how the platform tracks consumption

Powered by **Red Hat AI Factory with NVIDIA** - enterprise-grade AI infrastructure running on Red Hat OpenShift AI.

Let's get started.
